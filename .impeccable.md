## Design Context

### Users
- Internal researchers and tool-selection teammates who need to quickly discover AI products and jump into official community groups.
- They usually arrive with a product name, keyword, domain, or GitHub repository and want to verify relevance fast.
- The main job to be done is efficient scanning: search, compare, identify the most useful result, and scan the QR code immediately.

### Brand Personality
- Professional, efficient, focused.
- Calm and confidence-building rather than flashy.
- Feels like a search tool for fast internal research, not a marketing landing page.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Search-engine-like minimal light theme.
- QR-code-first result cards with compact metadata and low-noise supporting UI.
- Reference feeling: high-efficiency Chinese developer search pages.
- Anti-reference: oversized hero cards, decorative dashboards, stacked nested cards, and generic AI startup visuals.

### Design Principles
- Put search first: the input, primary action, and filters should dominate the page.
- Make QR codes the visual anchor of results; supporting metadata should stay compact.
- Reduce visual noise through tighter copy, lighter surfaces, and fewer competing containers.
- Use progressive disclosure for secondary tools such as viewed history and manual upload.
- Motion should be subtle, purposeful, and accessibility-safe.
